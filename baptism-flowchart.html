THE BASIS
What must be seen before the water
"Therefore we were buried with Him through baptism into death, that just as Christ was raised from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life."
ROMANS 6:4
A wife would never bury her husband if she believed there was even a hint of life left in him. You only bury the dead when it is beyond doubt that they are dead.
Baptism is the same. You do not go into the water hoping to finish something off. You go in when you have seen — beyond doubt — that the old man was crucified.

Romans 6 — The Logic of Freedom
