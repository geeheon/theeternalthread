THE KINGDOM OF GOD

Not a place. Not a programme. A reign.

βασιλεία τοῦ θεοῦ — basileia tou theou
WHAT IS THE KINGDOM?
The Kingdom of God is not primarily a location — it is the active reign of God through Christ. Wherever Christ is Lord — actually governing, actually obeyed, actually expressed — there the Kingdom is present. It is not a place you go to. It is a reality that comes to you when the King takes His rightful place.

Heaven when you die

A denomination
Social justice alone

A territory on a map
IT IS
God's active reign now
Christ as actual Lord
Righteousness, peace, joy
Within you / among you
Present and coming
Both now and not yet
Wherever the King rules
THE ALREADY / NOT YET TENSION
ALREADY
The King has come
The Spirit indwells
Sin's power broken
New creation begun
Kingdom entered at new birth
Christ reigns in believers
NOT YET
The King will return
All enemies under His feet
Death destroyed
Creation fully renewed
Kingdom manifested visibly
Every knee bows
We live in the overlap — the Kingdom is here but not yet fully here.
This tension is where faith operates.
"For the kingdom of God is not eating and drinking, but righteousness and peace and joy in the Holy Spirit."
ROMANS 14:17

The Kingdom is not a thing you build.
It is a Person whose reign you submit to.
Wherever He is Lord — in a heart,
in a home, in a gathering —
the Kingdom is present.

Seek first this Kingdom.
Everything else follows.
