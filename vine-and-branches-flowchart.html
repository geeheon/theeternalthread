THE VINE AND ABIDING
THE WARNING
THE FRUIT

Abiding is not anxious self-maintenance. It is the branch's natural posture — remaining in the vine, receiving life, bearing what the vine produces.

The moment you begin striving to abide, you have already stopped abiding.

A branch attached to the vine
does not worry about fruit.
It simply stays.
The life does the rest.
