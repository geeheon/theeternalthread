THE GROUND OF RECKONING

The Christian life does not begin by effort from Adam toward Christ, but from the reality of already being in Christ by new birth.

Reckoning is not making something true. It is counting as true what already is.

ROMANS 6:11 RESTS ON ROMANS 5:12–21

You did not choose to be born in Adam.
You cannot earn your way into Christ.
One birth placed you. Another birth transferred you.
Now — reckon it true.
